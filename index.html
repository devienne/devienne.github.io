Welcome to my website!

As a physicist, I have always been fascinated by (the apparently magic) magnetic materials. In my research, I use 3D finite-element micromagnetic modeling, coupled with machine learning tools and experimental work to understand how magnetic minerals interact with biological molecules, and how such interaction may have been fundamental for life's appearance. I got a PhD in Geophysics from Peking University in 2024, and during my PhD I was also a Visiting Researcher at the University of Edinburgh (UK).
